Q) Do you have any ambitions towards raiding for the Cutting Edge achievement each tier?

A) Maybe some day, but not right now.

Q) OK, well, what about Ahead of the Curve?

A) We do intend on achieving AOTC during each raid tier. We believe that the current state of the game makes this easily attainable regardless of raid composition. Exceptional players should have no difficulty with this level of raiding, and newer players shouldn't have much trouble learning. The key is to be coachable!

Q) How many officers and/or class leaders do you have?

Q) What is the atmosphere in your guild like?

A) We like to joke around during downtime, but we expect raiding time to be serious business, particularly when we're progressing through a new tier. It's important to keep in mind that we have a limited amount of time each week to try to get through an entire raid, and that any time we squander early on will have a non-trivial impact on how we perform during the rest of the tier. Once we have content on farm, then we can commence with memes and general mayhem. However, until that time comes, focus in raids will be our top priority.

Q) I'm interested in applying to your guild! What does the ideal candidate look like in your mind?

A) The ideal candidate will be brought along to every raid and will be constantly sought after for mythic+ runs. They will have a sense of humor, as well as a strong desire to constantly learn and improve. We require a few addons for raiding, but you likely will already possess most, if not all of them.

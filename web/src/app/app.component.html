About

<The Nomads> has been a guild since the final tier of Legion. Many of us have been friends with one another for longer than that. The founding members started as Alliance on the Earthen Ring server. Over time, we found the atmosphere of the server to be unsatisfactory, and the raiding scene to be nonexistent. Eventually, enough of us became disgruntled enough to be willing to leave the server in search of greener pastures. We eventually settled on Thrall, and at the end of Antorus, we made the switch.

Most of us completely re-rolled characters when we formed on Thrall. Despite this, and with outside assistance, we managed to re-clear and attain AOTC in Antorus before 8.0. While we had ambitions for Battle for Azeroth, it seems as though the folks at Activision-Blizzard had other plans. As a result, the majority of our base quit the game. It just wasn't fun to them anymore.

Now, we're reforming for Shadowlands. While the beta hasn't delivered on everything we wanted, we feel that it will be at least sufficient enough to warrant forming a raiding guild again.

For any questions, please check out our FAQ. You can also message Munsy#11430 on Battle.net.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q) What does your raid schedule look like?

A) We raid on Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 6:00 PM (Pacific/Standard) to 9:00 PM (Pacific/Standard). The remaining days can be used for optional alt raids and endless amounts of Torghast/Mythic Plus.

We are highly unlikely to deviate from this schedule.

Q) Do you have any ambitions towards raiding for the Cutting Edge achievement each tier?

A) Maybe some day, but not right now.

Q) OK, well, what about Ahead of the Curve?

A) We do intend on achieving AOTC during each raid tier. We believe that the current state of the game makes this easily attainable regardless of raid composition. Exceptional players should have no difficulty with this level of raiding, and newer players shouldn't have much trouble learning. The key is to be coachable!

Q) Who is your guild master?

A) Munsy.

Q) Who is your raid leader?

A) Blaph.

Q) How many officers and/or class leaders do you have?

A) We have two officers: Blaph and Xavill. We don't have class leaders and probably never will.

Q) What is the atmosphere in your guild like?

A) We like to joke around during downtime, but we expect raiding time to be serious business, particularly when we're progressing through a new tier. It's important to keep in mind that we have a limited amount of time each week to try to get through an entire raid, and that any time we squander early on will have a non-trivial impact on how we perform during the rest of the tier. Once we have content on farm, then we can commence with memes and general mayhem. However, until that time comes, focus in raids will be our top priority.

Q) I'm interested in applying to your guild! What does the ideal candidate look like in your mind?

A) The ideal candidate will be brought along to every raid and will be constantly sought after for mythic+ runs. They will have a sense of humor, as well as a strong desire to constantly learn and improve. We require a few addons for raiding, but you likely will already possess most, if not all of them.
